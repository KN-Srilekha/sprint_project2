ABOUT US


Cone Land : An fine dine dessert brand of Sri Foods serves fusion ice
creams blended by our creative chefs on a cold stone using fresh fruits, eggless
pastries and condiments served with love and affection by our happy staffs who look
forward to creating an ice cream memories.

Our journey in to food concepts started in Sep 2003 when our Founder
Srilekha – An food enthusiast, incorporated Sri Foods
with an Vision to build India’s largest concept food chain. Incorporated in Feb
2004, Sri Foods started with a Mission to serve high quality hand crafted food
by extremely motivated and customer friendly staff who would serve extra mile
to put a smile on the face of their customers.

With a clear expansion road map set by its Board and driven by Goal focussed
employees, Sri Foods continues to deliver high customer satisfaction &
shareholder value

OUR FLAVOURS


Ice cream, frozen dairy food made from cream or butterfat, milk, sugar, and flavourings.

Frozen custard and French-type ice creams also contain eggs.

Hundreds of flavours have been devised, the most popular being vanilla, chocolate, and strawberry.

Most people have their favourite flavors-some common ones being the timeless Vanilla, the Evergreen mango, the quintessential Butterscotch, the all-time favourite Chocolate,
and the chic and stylish Cassarta. But over the years the palate of the people has evolved and encashing on that many ice cream brands we have come up with our unique flavours
